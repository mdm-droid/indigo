### Overview

Sacred Journey: Trivia Quest is an immersive, educational trivia game designed to help players master historical and spiritual facts about eight major Manifestations of God: Abraham, Moses, Krishna, Buddha, Zoroaster, Jesus Christ, Muḥammad, and The Báb.

Drawing directly from a comprehensive study guide, the game transforms academic content into a "quest" through different spiritual realms, encouraging retention through interactive feedback and strategic gameplay.

### Key Features

* Eight Unique Realms: The game is organized into chapters based on the religious figures. As players progress, the "Realm" badge updates to show which part of the journey they are currently navigating.

* Comprehensive Question Pool: The game features 40 multiple-choice questions, ensuring that every detail from the source document—from birthplaces to central teachings—is covered.

* "Divine Insight" Hint System: Players are provided with three hints per game. Using a hint automatically eliminates two incorrect options, providing a strategic 50/50 advantage for challenging questions.

* Heart-Based Life System: To balance the challenge, players start with 5 hearts. This allows room for learning without immediate failure, encouraging a growth mindset.

* Instant Educational Feedback: When a player answers incorrectly, the game displays a modal containing the specific fact from the document. This ensures that the player learns the correct information immediately before moving forward.

* Visual Progress Tracking: A dynamic progress bar at the top of the screen fills as players move toward the 100% completion goal.

* Atmospheric Design: The interface uses a "glassmorphism" aesthetic with a deep celestial gradient and the "Cinzel" serif font to create a tone that is both modern and reverent.

### Educational Objective

The primary goal of the game is to provide a self-paced, engaging way for students or curious minds to internalize the lives and missions of these historical figures, rewarding accuracy and providing a safety net for discovery.
